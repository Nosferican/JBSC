  Puerto Rico is an U.S. unincorporated territory and its political situation, economic downturn, migration patterns, and technological capabilities create a unique combination of challenges and opportunities for the healthcare system. This study investigates the state of healthcare information technology adoption in Puerto Rico and provides policy recommendations for implementation aligned with local institutional context.
